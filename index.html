About The Conference

Slogan: “Integrating Knowledge for a Sustainable and Resilient Future”

Building on the remarkable success of RCST 2024, the Central Campus of Technology (CCT), Tribhuvan University, Hattisar, Dharan, is delighted to announce the Second Regional Conference on Science and Technology (RCST 2026), to be held on 5–6 February 2026. This two-day multidisciplinary conference will bring together faculty members, researchers, and students from across eastern Nepal to share knowledge, exchange ideas, and discuss recent advancements in science and technology.
We invite contributions from a wide range of fields, including Physical Sciences, Biological Sciences, Food and Nutrition, Statistics and Mathematics, Computer Science, and Information Technology. Faculty members engaged in research or doctoral studies, as well as students involved in thesis or project work, are strongly encouraged to submit and present their research findings, innovative ideas, and projects.
The main objective of RCST 2026 is to provide a platform for brilliant minds from eastern Nepal to showcase their work, foster discussions, and generate new ideas within the scientific community. This conference also aims to strengthen collaborations among faculty, researchers, and students, promoting a vibrant network for future scientific endeavors.

For inquiries and further information, please contact: rcst2026@gmail.com

CALL FOR ABSTRACTS

RCST 2026 warmly invites contributions from diverse disciplines, including Physical Sciences, Biological Sciences, Food and Nutrition, Statistics and Mathematics, Computer Science, and Information Technology. Researchers and students are encouraged to submit abstracts based on completed or ongoing work for consideration as either oral or poster presentations.
All submissions will be reviewed by the Scientific Review Committee, which will decide the mode of presentation. Authors will be informed of the acceptance status and presentation type via email.

FAQ's

Q: How long should my abstract be?
A: Abstracts should typically be between 200–350 words, unless otherwise specified.

Q: Can I submit more than one abstract?
A: Yes, multiple submissions are allowed, provided each is original and relevant.
